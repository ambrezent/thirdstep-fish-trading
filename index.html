How it Works

From ocean to your doorstep — here's exactly what happens after you click "Add to cart".

01

Browse & choose your fish

Browse today's catch — each listing shows the fish name in English and Arabic, the day's price, and availability (in stock or pre-order). Click any card to open the full detail view.

02

Select your cut & add notes

Choose from 6 cut formats — whole, cleaned, fillet, steaks, curry cut, or custom. Add any special instructions: thickness, split head for soup, skin-on or skin-off, anything you need.

Whole uncut Cleaned & gutted Fillet skin-on Steaks Curry cut Custom
03

Add to cart & choose delivery

Add multiple fish to your cart, adjust quantities, and select add-ons like ice packs or insulated boxes. Then choose your preferred delivery window — morning or afternoon, up to 3 days ahead.

04

Order via WhatsApp — it's instant

Your full order — fish, cut, notes, delivery date, address — is automatically formatted into a WhatsApp message. One tap sends it directly to our team. No app download, no account needed.

Cut options explained

🐟
Whole uncut
Fish delivered exactly as caught. No cleaning, no scaling. Ideal if you prefer to prepare it yourself or for display.
✂️
Cleaned & gutted
Gills and innards removed, fish scaled. Ready to cook whole on the grill or in a pan. Head and tail intact.
🍽️
Fillet skin-on
Two boneless fillets with skin intact. Ideal for grilling, baking, or pan-frying. Crispy skin results guaranteed.
🔪
Steaks
Cross-cut slices through the whole fish. Standard thickness ~2.5cm unless you specify otherwise in notes.
🍲
Curry cut
Smaller, irregular pieces cut specifically for UAE-style fish curry. Bones in for maximum flavour in the gravy.
💬
Custom
Describe exactly what you need in the special instructions field. Our fishmonger will do his best to accommodate.

Frequently asked questions

What time do you deliver?

We offer two delivery windows daily: 8am–1pm and 2pm–7pm, Saturday to Thursday. Select your preferred window during checkout. We'll send a WhatsApp message when we're 30 minutes away.

How do I pay?

We accept cash on delivery only. Our driver will collect payment when your order is handed over. We're working on card and digital wallet payments — coming soon.

What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver across Abu Dhabi city, Khalifa City, Al Reem Island, Yas Island, and Saadiyat Island. Delivery to Al Ain and Sharjah is available for orders above AED 200.

What if I'm not happy with my order?

Your satisfaction is guaranteed. If the quality isn't what you expected, message us on WhatsApp within 2 hours of delivery and we'll arrange a replacement or full refund — no questions asked.

Can I order for bulk or corporate events?

Absolutely. We cater for weddings, Majlis events, restaurants, and corporate gatherings. Message us directly on WhatsApp for bulk pricing and scheduling.

What is a pre-order?

Some fish are seasonal or depend on daily catches. Pre-order items are reserved for the next boat return, typically 48–72 hours. You'll be notified on WhatsApp the moment your fish lands.

Our Story

We started FreshCatch to fix something that bothered us: why does buying fresh fish in Abu Dhabi feel like a gamble?

From fishermen to families

FreshCatch was founded in 2024 by a team of Abu Dhabi locals who grew up buying fish from the Mina Zayed market — and watching it become harder and harder to find genuinely fresh, locally caught seafood at a fair price.

We partner directly with 14 fishing families across Abu Dhabi, eliminating every middleman between the ocean and your table. The fishermen set the price, and we handle everything from collection to delivery.

Our model is simple: orders placed before 6pm are processed the following morning, cleaned to your specification, and delivered in the same day. No frozen imports. No mystery fish. No guessing.
